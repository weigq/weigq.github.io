Guoqiang Wei (魏国强)

Ph.D. candidate

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Electronic Enginerring & Information Science (EEIS). I am involved in the joint Ph.D. program between USTC and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), luckily advised by Yan Lu (MSRA), Prof. Zhibo Chen (USTC) and Wenjun Zeng (EIAS). I collaborate closely with Zhizheng Zhang and Cuiling Lan during my internship at Intelligent Multimedia group (IM), MSRA.

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in the areas of domain adaptation/gerneralization, transfer learning, and pose estimation.
