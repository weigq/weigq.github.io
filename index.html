Guoqiang Wei (魏国强)

Ph.D. candidate

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Electronic Enginerring & Information Science (EEIS). I am involved in the joint PhD program between USTC and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), luckily advised by Prof. Zhibo Chen from USTC and Prof. Wenjun Zeng from MSRA. Before that, I received my B.Eng from USTA in 2018. I collaborate closely with Dr. Cuiling Lan during my internship at Intelligent Multimedia group (IM), MSRA.

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in the areas of domain adaptation/gerneralization, transfer learning, and pose estimation.
